Top 3 movies according to Ashwin

Here is a list of top 3 movies I have watched so far

Inception

An exceptional must-watch movie which explores the concepts of dream state and how dreams can be manipulated

Fight club

Another great movie, let's you get acquainted with themes such as identity, masculinity, self-exploration, consumerism culture, etc.

Blade runner 2049

This movie is set in a dystopian future, explores themes of idenity, loneliness, sense of purpose, future of humanity and many more.
